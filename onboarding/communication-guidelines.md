# Communication Guidelines

---

## Principles
- Be respectful
- Be professional
- Ask questions when stuck
- Communicate blockers early

---

## Weekly Expectations
- Attend meetings
- Update task status
- Submit weekly reports

---

## Technical Discussions
Focus on:
- Facts
- Data
- Technical reasoning

Avoid:
- Personal criticism
- Unprofessional language

---

## Escalation Path
- Contributor  
  ↓  
- Mentor  
  ↓  
- Project Lead  
  ↓  
- Program Coordinator
