# Roadmap

---

## Vision
- Build an open, community-driven ecosystem where students, researchers, and engineers collaborate to create software, AI agents, and distributed infrastructure while gaining real-world engineering experience.
- Our long-term goal is to create a sustainable platform that:
- - Trains industry-ready engineers
- - Supports research and innovation
- - Promotes open-source collaboration
- - Enables community-powered infrastructure
- - Accelerates software development through AI and automation

---

## Strategic Objectives
- Education – Develop a structured learning pathway that bridges academic education and industry practices
- Open Source – Build and maintain community-owned open-source projects
- Infrastructure – Create scalable cloud-native infrastructure for running distributed services and AI workloads
- Research – Support research scholars through technology, automation, and collaboration
- AI Automation – Develop AI agents that automate engineering and operational activities
